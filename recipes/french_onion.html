French Onion Breaded Baked chicken

Mix Breads crumps and dry soup mix together in a bowl

For as simple as this recipe was, it was pretty tasty. Dont fear the mayo-you cannot taste it!

The only change I made was to slighly pulse the onion soup mix in my food processor to get even texture

Ingredients

  • 1 cup bread crumps
  • 1 (1 ounce) package dry french onion soup mix
  • 1/3 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon garlic paste (optional)
  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breasts

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees
  2. Mix bread crumps and dry soup mix together in a bowl
  3. Mix mayonnaise and garlic paste together in a separate bowl
  4. Brush chicken breasts with the mayonnaise mixture
  5. Bake on the middle rack of the preheated oven until chicken is no longer pink in the center and juices run clear, about 20 minutes
